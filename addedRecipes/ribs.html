Smoked St. Louis Cut Pork Ribs


Description


These are St. Louis Cut Pork Ribs. They are a great way to make a meal for a big group of friends.


Ingredients


  • 1 Slab St. Louis Cut Pork Ribs
  • All Purpose (Salt, Pepper, Garlic) Rib/li>
  • Your favorite BBQ Flavor rub
  • 1 stick of butter
  • Your favorite BBQ Sauce
  • Apple Cider Vinegar (in a spray bottle)

Directions


  1. Trim excess fat and small pointed ends from the rack
  2. remove the silver-skin from the bone side of the rack
  3. Using a paper towel, pat off excess moisture
  4. Apply your general purpose rub and pat into the meat on both sides
  5. After allowing to rest for 10 minutes, apply your BBQ rub generously to all sides and pat in
  6. Smoke directly on rack of a wood-burning smoker for 2 hours, spritizing with vinegar every 30 minutes
  7. Slice butter crosswise along the stick into quarter inch slices
  8. measure out tin foil large enough in which the ribs may be securely wrapped
  9. place butter in a single file line at center of wrap and pour 1/2 cup BBQ sauce over them
  10. place ribs meat side down over the butter/sauce and wrap completely in foil
  11. Place ribs back on smoker meat side down and cook until internal temp hits 195deg F
  12. remove ribs and open wrap. Flip ribs to meat side up and brush lightly with sauce
  13. Sprinkle a bit of rub across the entire top of the rack but go lightly!
  14. Fold sides of wrap around ribs to form a small pan and place ribs back on smoker for 15 minutes
  15. Remove ribs from rack and allow to rest for 30 minutes. Slice between each bone and serve.
